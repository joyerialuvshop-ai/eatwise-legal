EatWise Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-03-03

This Privacy Policy explains how EatWise ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the EatWise mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Profile & Health Data

The App allows you to enter profile information such as age, height, weight, goals, meal logs, water intake, and weight entries. This data is primarily stored locally on your device.

Food Photos

If you choose to log meals by photo, images may be processed using AI services to estimate nutritional information. Photo logging is optional.

Device & Usage Data

We may collect limited device and diagnostic information such as device model, operating system version, app version, crash reports, performance data, and interaction events to improve the reliability and performance of the App.

2. Advertising & Tracking

The App may display advertisements, including banner and native ads.

We may use advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These partners may collect or receive device identifiers (such as Advertising ID on Android or IDFA on iOS) and other technical information to provide and measure advertisements.

On iOS devices, we request permission for tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing the device advertising identifier for personalized ads.

If you decline tracking or consent where applicable, non-personalized ads may still be shown.

For users in certain regions (including the EEA and UK), we may request consent for personalized advertising through a consent management platform (e.g., Google UMP). If consent is not granted, only non-personalized ads will be served where possible.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to support certain features of the App, including:

  • Advertising services such as Google AdMob
  • Analytics
  • Crash reporting and diagnostics services such as Sentry
  • AI-based food analysis
  • These providers may process limited technical data necessary for their functionality.

These services may process limited technical data necessary to provide their functionality.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Most user profile and nutrition tracking data is stored locally on your device. Some features that rely on third-party services may process limited data externally to provide functionality such as advertisements, crash reporting, or AI food analysis.

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the App’s functionality.

5. Children's Privacy

EatWise is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Data Rights

You may contact us to request deletion of any data associated with your use of the App where applicable.

7. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect information. However, no system is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
